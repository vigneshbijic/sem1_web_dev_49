Diwali th festivals of Lights

Diwali or Dipawali is Indias biggest and most important holiday ofthe year.the festival gets its from the row(avali) of clay lamps (deepa) that indians light outside their homes to symbozlize the inner light that protects from spiritual darkness.Diwali is known as the festival of light because diyas were lit on the day Lord Rama returned to Ayodhya.It symbolises the spiritual victory of Dharma over Adharma, light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance
