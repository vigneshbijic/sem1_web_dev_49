The college of engineering Chengannur(CEC),was established by the Government of kerala under the aegsis of the IHRD,in the year 1993.It has been approved by the ALL india council for Technical Education and affiliadet to APJ Abdul kalam Technological University(KTU).Ever since its inception,the college has maintained its eminence as a leading enginnering college in Kerala.being located in Chengannur town,Allepy district the college has got access to all means of transport ,communication and loging facilities.
